Weddings are filled with love and joy and everyone special in the couple’s life. But, for months leading up to the big day, a lot of decisions must be made by the happy couple and wedding favors are a big one!

Couples give out wedding favors to say thank you to their guests and provide a tangible memory of the special event. When choosing wedding favors, we recommend couples always start by determining the budget they feel comfortable with. Then, the couple needs to think about the type of party favor they’d like to give out – edible favors, keepsakes, etc. – and what would match well with the wedding theme (i.e., beach, rustic, glamorous, etc.).
