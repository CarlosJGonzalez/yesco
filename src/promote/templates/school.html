Are your students ready to go back to school?

When we think about how the school year ended, we know your students are ready to be back on campus engaging with their teachers and fellow students. Learning virtually just isn’t the same but we know the safety of your students is a priority on your campus this fall.

Fully Promoted has everything you need to welcome students back on campus. Why not greet them with a Back to School Care Package or stock your Student Store with new merchandise to build school spirit?
  • Pens, highlighters, pencils, etc. featuring your colors and logo are always popular with students
  • Hand sanitizer bottles featuring your mascot and a clip to make it easy to keep hands clean
  • Face masks embroidered or screen printed with your logo or mascot
  • Water bottles featuring your school name and a spot for students to write their name
  • Journals or notebooks in a variety of styles with your mascot and school name
  • Lanyards imprinted with your school name can hold student IDs making it easy to identify students
  • Drawstring bags designed to spotlight this year’s theme are great for carrying snacks, a sweatshirt, or extra art supplies
  
 
 
  Fully Promoted is ready to help you welcome your students back on campus – we can help you find just the right items to fit your student population and budget.
