DAS Group is working to make sure the images in your social media posts, blog posts, etc. are on brand and represent your products and services well. In working on these, we want to feature photos of projects you have created locally.

Please share your images with us! Locations that upload images by the end of March 2021 will automatically be entered for a chance to win an Amazon gift card valued at $150. The images you contribute must be approved for us to use on social media, blog posts, the website, ads, etc.
 
 
  1. Log in to LocalFullyPromoted
  2. Click on “Graphics Gallery” on the left
  3. Click on the 3 dots in the top right
  4. Select “Add Image”
  5. Complete the “Upload Image” box that pops up – see below
  6. Click on “Upload Image” when done
 
 
We need photos that are clear and high resolution (minimum of 1080 x 1080). Photos of all types of products are appreciated. When taking photos of projects, please take photos of the project up close as well as from different angles so we have even more variety. We would also like some photos of your local team on the job.

This is an ongoing need so whenever you have a few good images to share, please upload them to the Gallery.
